About Us

Four Girls Who Code students named In'Spire, Annie, Ashley, and Jayla wanted to create something that would positively impact students in California's Bay Area. As a result, they created Bay Owls, a social network that keeps students informed about the news and helps incoming freshmen choose what schools they want to go to based on reviews of the school from actual students. It also serves as a medium for people to connect to one another as well as a safe place.

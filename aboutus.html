About Us

Four Girls Who Code Students named In'Spire, Annie, Ashley, and Jayla decided to create a website that would impact students in the California's Bay Area. Bay Owls is a social network where people can go for advice. It also serves as a medium for people to connect to one another as well as a safe place. Additionally, it keeps students informed about the news and helps incoming freshmen choose what schools they want to go to based on reviews of the school from actual students.

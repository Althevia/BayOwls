Nutrition

Good nutrition is one of the keys to a healthy life. You can improve your health by keeping a balanced diet. Eat foods that contain vitamins and minerals.

Tips

Track your food intake every now and then
Eat more whole plants
Combine your food
Quality over quantity
Plan, prepare, and store healthy meals for the week
Get to know your body
Make time to eat with those you love

The Do's

Eat vegetables
Use herbs and spices
Eat fruits
Get enough protein
Eat fatty fish
Take care of your gut health with probiotics and fiber
Drink water
Use extra virgin olive oil (healthiest fat)
Eat nuts

The Don'ts

Don't drink sugar calories
Avoid processed junk food
Don't fear coffee
Don't overcook or burn meat
Don't eat a lot of refined carbohydrates
Don't fear saturated fat
Minimize intake of added sugars
Avoid artificial trans fat
Don't go on a diet
